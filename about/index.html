About

TL;DR, I am a: junior Rails developer, bartender, Liverpool FC fan (YNWA!!), reformed recording engineer, lover of tech, brother, uncle, introvert, Scorpio, gym rat!

I'm pretty hopeless when it comes to describing myself, but here is a little about me.

I was born in Jacksonville, Florida. I am the youngest of three boys and I grew up playing sports. I even played ice hockey, yes in Florida. My college experience was unique. I went to two completely different schools, struggling to find a career I could be passionate about. I have a bachelor's degree from the University of North Florida in Community Health. What's that you might ask? Long story....I also have an associate's degree in Recording Arts from Full Sail University. I know I know, huh? I wanted to dj since the seventh grade (when I saw my first pair of real life SL-1200's!!) and dabbled with it some years later. Music pushed me to forgo a nursing career (the whole B.S. in Community Health thing), which brings me to now...

I moved to Atlanta in 2010 and have been pursuing a career in the recording industry since (while moonlighting as a bartender). These past few years have been the toughest I've experienced so far in my life, but also have taught me a great deal. I've met some famous Atlanta artists as well as a famous French house DJ (ask me about a story involvoing food allergies and said dj). As you might expect, getting a job in the music industry is not easy!

At the moment I am in the process of a career change. I'm moving into the web development field and just finished training at The Iron Yard (learning Ruby on Rails). I am super excited about this journey and looking forward to starting my first job! I've always loved tech and have used a computer since a young age.

Wow! You made it this far! I trust my story was compelling and worth your time. Feel free to browse my portfolio and experience if you want to know more about my work.
